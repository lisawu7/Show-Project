Penthouse

The Penthouse: War in Life[7] (Korean: 펜트하우스; RR: Penteuhauseu; lit. Penthouse) is a South Korean television series starring Lee Ji-ah, Kim So-yeon, Eugene,[8] Um Ki-joon, Yoon Jong-hoon, and Park Eun-seok.[9][10] The series, directed by Joo Dong-min and written by Kim Soon-ok,[11] spins the story of a real estate and education war, a desire to be number one. It depicts the solidarity and revenge of women who turned to evilness to protect themselves and their children.[12][13] It premiered on SBS TV on October 26, 2020.[14]
